55 to 65 days are needed from germination to harvest of the root.

The usually deep purple roots of beetroot are eaten either grilled,boilded, or roasted as a cooked vegetable, cold as a salad after cooking and adding oil and vinegar, or raw and shreadded, either alone or combinded with any salad vegetable.
